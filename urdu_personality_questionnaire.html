Hissa A: Tumhari Asli Shakhsiyat (15 sawal)
Yeh sawaal tumhari basic personality traits ko samajhne ke liye hain
A1. Main naturally outgoing aur social type ka banda/bandi hoon, logon se milna julna pasand hai.
A2. Main cheezon ko plan karke karna pasand karta/karti hoon, last minute ya random kaam pasand nahi.
A3. Main chhoti chhoti baaton ko bhi bohat sochta/sochti rehta hoon, overthinking hoti hai.
A4. Main generally calm rehta/rehti hoon aur stress ko achay se handle kar leta/leti hoon.
A5. Mujhe nayi cheezain try karna aur different experiences lena pasand hai.
A6. Main doosron ki feelings aur zarooraton ka bohat khayal rakhta/rakhti hoon.
A7. Main bohat disciplined hoon aur apne wadon/commitments ko follow through karta/karti hoon.
A8. Mujhe doosron ki criticism/tanqeed se bohat takleef hoti hai, khass kar apnon se.
A9. Mujhe lead karna aur charge lena comfortable lagta hai.
A10. Main apni asli feelings ko doosron se chhupa leta/leti hoon.
A11. Jab main stress mein hota/hoti hoon, to future ke bare mein bohat negative sochta/sochti hoon.
A12. Jab koi mujhe confront kare to main defensive ho jata/jati hoon.
A13. Mujhe mushkil kaam ko complete karne tak tikte rehna difficult lagta hai.
A14. Main aksar doosron ki expectations poori karte karte khud thak jata/jati hoon.
A15. Mujhe apne emotions ki clear samajh hai - main jaanta/jaanti hoon ke main kya feel kar raha/rahi hoon aur kyun.
Hissa B: Rishte Mein Tumhari Priorities (12 sawal)
Tumhare liye rishte mein sab se zyada kya important hai?
B1. Mere liye rishte mein emotional connection aur samajhdar hona sab se important hai.
B2. Physical attraction aur looks mere liye bohat important hain partner mein.
B3. Main character aur values ko zyada priority deta/deti hoon looks ya status se.
B4. Main chahta/chahti hoon ke mera partner mera best friend bhi ho, sirf romantic partner nahi.
B5. Mere partner ka mere family ke sath achay se rahna mere liye bohat zaroori hai.
B6. Shared religious/spiritual values mere liye rishte mein essential hain.
B7. Main rishte ke decisions zyada logic se leta/leti hoon, emotions se nahi.
B8. Mere partner ki education level aur career goals mujhe bohat matter karte hain.
B9. Mujhe apne partner se regular reassurance aur pyar ka izhar chahiye.
B10. Mera mann hai ke ek achha partner mujh se bohat zyada disagree ya challenge na kare.
B11. Financial stability aur paisa manage karna mere liye top priority hai partner mein.
B12. Main chahta/chahti hoon mera partner mujhe space aur independence de, overly clingy na ho.
Hissa C: Emotional Attachment ka Style (10 sawal)
Tum emotionally kaise connect hote ho aur closeness ko kaise handle karte ho
C1. Mujhe rishton mein rejected ya chhora jane ka bohat darr rehta hai.
C2. Main rishton mein bohat jaldi emotionally attach ho jata/jati hoon.
C3. Agar mujhe lagay ke koi door ho raha hai, to main double effort lagata/lagati hoon unko paas rakhne ke liye.
C4. Jab koi qareebi naraaz ho, main bohat guilty feel karta/karti hoon chahe meri galti na bhi ho.
C5. Main apne asli emotions share karne se ghabrata/ghabrati hoon ke doosre kya sochenge.
C6. Jab rishte mein koi problem ho, main seedha address karta/karti hoon avoid nahi karta/karti.
C7. Main conflict se bachna prefer karta/karti hoon chahe apni feelings express na kar sakun.
C8. Main aksar romantic relationships mein jealous ya insecure feel karta/karti hoon.
C9. Mujhe apne partner ki full attention chahiye, uncomfortable lagta hai jab wo doosron pe focus kare.
C10. Main vulnerable aur open hona comfortable feel karta/karti hoon romantic partners ke sath.
Hissa D: Decisions Lena aur Bahar ka Pressure (10 sawal)
Tum important faislay kaise lete ho aur bahar ke pressure ko kaise handle karte ho
D1. Mere liye family ki approval meri apni pasand se zyada important hai bari decisions mein.
D2. Mujhe "log kya kahenge" ka bohat pressure feel hota hai mere choices ke bare mein.
D3. Mujhe bohat darr lagta hai ke galat decision lo aur log criticism karein.
D4. Main apne decisions pe firm reh sakta/sakti hoon chahe family ya dost strongly disagree karein.
D5. Barte hue, zyada tar important decisions mere elders ne liye, maine zyada follow kiya.
D6. Main aksar doosron ki khushi ko apni khushi pe priority deta/deti hoon.
D7. Mere liye apni awaz rakhna bohat mushkil hai jab authority figures ki strong opinions hon.
D8. Agar koi decision mujhe emotionally barbaad karega, main us se nikal sakta/sakti hoon family pressure ke bawajood.
D9. Main chahta/chahti hoon ke log mujhe funny, smart, ya likeable samjhein, agar nahi samjhein to mujhe bothers karta hai.
D10. Main major life decisions primarily us base pe leta/leti hoon jo mujhe personally sahi lagta hai.
Hissa E: Commitment aur Rishton ki History (12 sawal)
Tumhare past relationships mein kaisa pattern raha hai
E1. Mere paas relationships shuru karne lekin serious hone se pehle chhor dene ki history hai.
E2. Kisi ko pursue karna actual relationship se zyada appealing lagta hai mujhe.
E3. Jab mujhe koi cheez ya koi mil jaye jo main chahta/chahti tha, main interest kho deta/deti hoon.
E4. Main kabhi bhi kisi mushkil decision pe extended period tak committed nahi raha/rahi.
E5. Main hamesha ek "escape route" apne dimaag mein rakhta/rakhti hoon committed situations mein bhi.
E6. Maine past mein logon ko hurt kiya hai achanak relationships khatam kar ke bina proper communication ke.
E7. Jab relationships real aur serious ho jati hain, mujhe suffocate feel hota hai.
E8. Maine successfully apni life mein kam az kam ek long-term committed relationship maintain ki hai.
E9. Mujhe yakeen hai main life partner ke sath commit kar sakta/sakti hoon agar sahi insaan mile.
E10. Main aksar current partners ko past interests ya fantasies se compare karta/karti hoon.
E11. Main jo nahi mil sakta use chahna aur jo mil jaye us ki value kho dena - yeh mera struggle hai.
E12. Mere paas relationships ke bohat mauke aaye lekin main zyada tar se walk away kar gaya/gayi.
Hissa F: Empathy aur Apne Aap Pe Focus (8 sawal)
Tum apni zarooraton ko doosron ki feelings ke sath kaise balance karte ho
F1. Main tab hi doosron ki madad karta/karti hoon jab mujhe kuch fayda mile.
F2. Har situation mein main automatically dekhta/dekhti hoon ke mujhe sab se zyada kya benefit hoga.
F3. Doosron ka dard ya khushi mujhe affect nahi karti jab tak mujhe directly impact na ho.
F4. Main genuinely logon ki parwaah karta/karti hoon aur aksar unki zarooraton ko apni se pehle rakhta/rakhti hoon.
F5. Main easily imagine kar sakta/sakti hoon ke doosre kaise feel kar rahe hain aur accordingly apna behavior adjust karta/karti hoon.
F6. Mujhe samajh nahi aata ke log cheezon pe itne emotional kyun ho jate hain.
F7. Main willingly apna comfort sacrifice karta/karti hoon kisi ki genuine zaroorat ko help karne ke liye.
F8. Logon ne mujhe kabhi kabhi selfish ya self-centered kaha hai.
Hissa G: Control aur Power (8 sawal)
Rishton mein control aur authority ke sath tumhara comfort level
G1. Mujhe pasand hai ke main hi rishte mein final decisions lun.
G2. Main prefer karta/karti hoon ke mera partner mujh se agree kare rather than challenge kare.
G3. Mujhe bother hota hai jab mera partner doosron pe attention de mujh pe focus karne ki bajaye.
G4. Main chahta/chahti hoon ke mera partner feel kare ke main unke liye sab kuch hoon.
G5. Mera yakeen hai equal decision-making aur power sharing mein relationships mein.
G6. Mujhe uncomfortable feel hota hai jab mujhe lagta hai main control mein nahi hoon.
G7. Meri ego jaldi hurt ho jati hai jab mera partner meri opinions validate nahi karta/karti.
G8. Main respect aur welcome karta/karti hoon apne partner ki independence aur alag interests.
Hissa H: Paisa aur Practical Life Management (6 sawal)
Adult life ke practical aspects manage karne ki tumhari ability
H1. Main apna paisa achay se manage karta/karti hoon aur regularly save karta/karti hoon.
H2. Paisa mere hathon se easily slip ho jata hai - main track nahi rakhta/rakhti ke kahan ja raha hai.
H3. Main ek family ko financial security aur stability provide kar sakta/sakti hoon.
H4. Main apni cheezon, schedule, aur responsibilities ke sath organized hoon.
H5. Main stress aur unexpected problems ko calmly aur effectively handle karta/karti hoon.
H6. Main household aur family responsibilities manage karne ke capable hoon.
